Mahabaleshwar Agro Village

Located at the base of a valley on the banks of the vast expanse of the Koyna backwaters sorrounded by the hills of Sahyadris, Mahabaleshwar Agro Village is a perfect destination to enjoy some quality time with your family and friends. With spacious secure tents pitched along side the banks equipped with flush toilets and all the amenities to ensure a comfortable stayMahabaleshwarAgro Village offer a beautiful off beat stay option completely different from other holiday experiences. With arguable the largest campus amongst any hotels in Tapola or resorts in Tapola, there is no dearth of open spaces in Mahabaleshwar Agro Village.

An interesting thing to notice is that 3 other rivers come out from the cow's mouth apart from Krishna and they all travel some distance before merging into Krishna which flows East towards the Bay of Bengal. These rivers are the Koyna, Venna (Veni) and Gayatri. The Savitri river flows Westward via Mahad to the Arabian Sea.
