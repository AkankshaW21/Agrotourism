Ekaant The Retreat

Ekaant the retreat. A charming 20 room resort, perched high amidst the Sahyadri hills, and get a taste of peace & solitude, in lush Green Valley, overlooking the back waters of the 20 km long Warasgaon Lake with panoramic view of the rolling hills & winding lake.



Ekaant means Solitude is a small 20 room holiday resort perched on one of the seven hills which surrounds the city of Lavasa. An ideal place for people who want to do nothing on a holiday. No site seeing, no trekking, no activity at all. It is meant for those who need to slow down their pace from the busy lives. It is for those who just want to relax!!!! Situated away from the main Lavasa city centre, it is right at the entrance of the city on the hill top and has some breathtaking views of the Warasgaon lake, has an ample parking space and you can witness the best sunset view right from your rooms.




Location:

Dasve Road Taluka: Mulshi, Pune, Lavasa 412112 India


Amenities:

- Free parking - Free High Speed Internet (WiFi) - Free breakfast - Children Activities (Kid / Family Friendly) - Business Centre with Internet Access - Conference facilities - Dry cleaning - Laundry service

KARE is a sanctuary that enables self renewal in an environment that can be described as an antithesis of our fast paced high tech world, bringing us into harmony with our natural state of being. The Rejuvenative journey includes Traditional Ayurvedic Massages, Detoxifying Therapies, Beautifying Spa Therapies*, individualized nutritious meals/ diet and the curative aspects of Iyengar Medical Yoga.

Sandipani Hometel

Sandipani Hometel is one of the best places in West Pune conveniently located one hour driving distance from Pune airport and 20 minutes from the Pune-Bangalore highway. Located in the picturesque setting on a hilltop admit lush green mountains at Lavale where family and friends can spend leisure time as well. It is part of Symbiosis International University campus. We have total 98 fully furnished comfortable rooms.
