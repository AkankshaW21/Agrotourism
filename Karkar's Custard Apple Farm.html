History

"Karkar's Custard apple Farm focuses on the production and distribution of farm-fresh Custard Apple for people globally through good agricultural practices. We ensure our entire range of products meets domestic and transnational quality standards, offering optimum quality at a price worth paying for our customers. We package all products with eco-friendly and safe materials, ensuring quality custard apples. Custard apple is a rich source of Vitamin C, an antioxidant that helps neutralize free radicals. It also contains Vitamin A, which benefits hair, eyes, and skin health. The magnesium in custard apples is essential for muscle relaxation and heart protection against diseases."
