Mantra Resort Pune

Mantra Resort is a picturesque hilltop retreat in the scenic hills near Pune, offering breathtaking views of the backwaters of Bhatghar Dam. Surrounded by lush green trees, the resort features a variety of accommodations, including cottages, bungalows, and studio rooms. Guests can enjoy a range of activities, including adventure rope courses, indoor and outdoor

games, trekking, and hiking. The resort also boasts a water park, swimming pool, and multiple dining options serving delicious vegetarian meals. Ideal for working couples and nuclear families seeking a fun-filled and adventurous vacation.




Location:

CTS No.63, Lal Bahadur Shastri Rd, Pune 411030 India


Amenities:

- Free parking - Pool - Bar / lounge - Archery - Badminton - Children's playground - Indoor play area for children - Car hire

13.4km

Nestled in the cocoon of the mighty Sahyadris, Encore –a Boutique Resort is a blissful, enchanting retreat miles away from the chaotic city life.

Residency Lake Resort

Residency Lake Resort & Spa, located in Mulshi 40 kms from Pune, is a brand new waterfront property. Maintaining its incomparable location in the Sahyadri Hills, the Residency Lake Resort & Spa not features better views, luxurious accommodations and state-of-the-art services. Offering a respite from the hustle and bustle of the city, this breathtaking property is a jewel in the Sahyadri Valley.

Residency Lake Resort & Spa overlooks the Mulshi lake and is surrounded by lofty hills and lush greenery - the perfect holiday destination for making memories. 12 cottages with plush interiors and mindful selected fittings and furnishings put you in the lap of luxury. Aesthetic lighting and colour schemes lend a touch of warmth and cosiness making you feel right at home.

Atmantan Wellness Resort

Atmantan Wellness Resort is a one of a kind, luxury wellness resort committed to transforming the Body, Mind and Soul through holistic practices. Nestled on a crystal hill, in the lap of the majestic Sahyadri mountain range; Atmantan Wellness Resort is spread over 42 luxuriant acres of valley, overlooking the picturesque Mulshi Lake, near Pune, India. The Atmantan Philosophy is to educate and inspire guests through enhanced lifestyle choices relating to food and healthy living. Every guest at Atmantan has the opportunity to learn, grow, create, improve and truly Be

Transformed! With retreat based programs at the heart of the Resort, guests are able to choose a program best suited to meet their individual goals. State of the art facilities at the resort includes: 106 vibrant rooms and villas, 3 food and beverage venues and over 60,000 square feet of wellness facility that offers result oriented programs with a multi-dimensional approach.
