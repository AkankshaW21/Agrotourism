About Us

Who Are We

We are students of Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune. From the IT department, C section, group 11.

At Explore Agro-Tourism, we believe that travel is not just about places; it's about immersing yourself in local traditions, experiencing rural life, and connecting with the land. Our mission is to offer unique agro-tourism experiences that allow you to explore rich agricultural heritage and vibrant rural communities.
We're committed to bridging the gap between visitors and the heart of farms and villages.
We provide carefully selected and categorized agro-tourism destinations, whether focused on organic farming, sustainable agriculture, or hands-on farm activities, to suit diverse interests and preferences.
Your experiences mean a lot to us. We value genuine visitor reviews as they offer valuable insights to others exploring agro-tourism.
After your visit, we encourage you to share your stories, tips, and feedback.
Your reviews help other travelers and contribute to the collective knowledge of rural tourism.

Our Mission

To be your trusted companion in exploring the scenic landscapes and diverse agricultural heritage of rural areas. We're dedicated to showcasing the most captivating agro-tourism destinations, from lush farmlands to quaint villages and everything in between. Our goal is to provide you with a seamless and memorable journey, whether you're an enthusiast of sustainable farming, a lover of rural culture, or simply curious about where your food comes from.
