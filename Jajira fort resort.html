Top Resorts Near Jajira


Golden Swan Beach Resort

Set on the outskirts of a fishing village, this relaxed, colonial-style beach resort is 5 km from ferry service to the 15th-century Murud-Janjira Fort and 26 km from Phansad Wildlife Sanctuary. Casual rooms feature colourful decor, and are set across the main resort and a beachfront villa. They include rustic wooden furnishings, flat-screen TVs and minifridges. Upgraded rooms and detached cottages add air-conditioning.

Breakfast is complimentary. There's a casual buffet restaurant and a garden featuring a kids' playground, as well as direct beach access.




Location:

Darbar Road, Opposite Custom Office, Murud Janjira, Murud, Maharashtra 402401


Amenities:

- Beach-front - Breakfast - free - Air conditioning - Wi-Fi


Distance from Jajira fort:

26km

Forest Hills at Tala

Offering forest and mountain views, this upscale hillside resort is 25 km from the NH66 and 2 km from the ancient Kuda Buddhist Caves. It's 29 km from the town of Murud. Ranging from stylish mud huts and permanent tents to rustic-chic tree houses and upcycled containers, all accommodations provide free Wi-Fi, smart TVs, and balconies or terraces. Upgraded units offer features such as plunge pools, hot tubs and/or open-air bathrooms. Room service is available.

Parking and meals are included. A casual restaurant and an outdoor infinity pool feature scenic views. There's also a petting zoo, hiking trails, and a spa area with treatment rooms.




Location:

Survey no. 157/9, Village-Taraste, Pitasai Kumbet Road Post-Pitsai, Taluka, Tala, Maharashtra 402111


Amenities:

- Pool -Spa -Parking -free -Breakfast free


Distance from sinhgad fort:

29km
